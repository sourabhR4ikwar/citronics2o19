CALL OF DUTY

1st Winner! : 10,000 Rs

2nd Winner! : 5,000 Rs

INTRODUCTION-

The foremost creed of a soldier is to accept challenges without conceding defeat. The call of duty asks for your bot to deploy, engage and overcome all the others thus being the conqueror. You require making your warriors tough and susceptible to walk through all the forbidden hurdles, rough beds, spinning floors and other odds and ends. Higher machinery may not be required but just a simple one which jumps, shoots and sprints in the best way possible. So train your little soldiers to survive through it all!

NO. OF PARTICIPANTS-

4 MEMBERS IN EACH TEAM

PROBLEM STATEMENT-

There are two bots competing against each other crossing the hurdles and henceforth leading the way to the next level. The machine should be capable of running on the track and completing the path in minimum time. The bots can be wired or wireless.

REGISTRATION FEE:

  • Rs 300/- per bot(Call of Duty)
  • Rs 750/- per bot(Call of Duty + Robo Race)
  • Rs 1000/- per bot(Call of Duty + Robo Race + Robo Soccer)

RE-REGISTRATION FEES-

  • Rs. 200/- per bot

Rules

First Level:

  • 2 bots will start together.
  • Time limit of the level is 5min.
  • Wherever the bot falls, it will start from the beginning of that level for only 3 times and bot is not allowed to skip any hurdle
  • The first bot to reach at the check point will be qualified to the next level.

Second Level:

  • Single bot will start from the check point of first level.
  • The second level is time based and also point based, each hurdle has different points.
  • Team to be selected for third level will be according to the point earned and if two or more bots have same points then they will be short listed according to time consumed.
  • Wherever the bot falls, it will start from the beginning of that level for only 2 times and there will be no disqualification even after the bot falls and time will be resumed.
  • There will be disqualification after the bot falls more than 2 times.
  • One Skip is allowed

Third Level:

  • Single bot will start from the end point of the second level.
  • The third level is also time based and point based.
  • The bot which falls will have to start again from the checkpoint of second level.
  • The winner of the event will be declared according to the point earned and if two or more bots have same points then they will be short listed according to time consumed.
  • The bot is allowed to skip hurdles once.
(NOTE: Rules can be altered on spot according to the situation.)

ROBOT SPECIFICATIONS-

  • Bot dimensions are less than 30x30x30 cm
  • Maximum power of 60W can be used
  • Maximum voltage of 24V can be used
  • Power supply provided will be DC only.
  • Bot connectivity shall be wired,wireless or autonomous

    • NOTE:-

    • Maximum 5% tolerance is allowable in power and voltage only.
    • Readymade toys car are not allowed.
    • The wire (for wired robots) should remain stack during the complete run.
    • We will provide on board 230V, 50Hz AC power supply.
    • Bring your own AC to DC converters or own Batteries any type(li-ion,lead acid etc).
    • Robots must be insulated for particular hurdles.
